The Slim Booth

The Slim Booth is one of the sleakest booths in our inventory. With a built in camera mount it takes HD pictures with a DSLR camera and has a large 42" touch screen. Guests can choose from a 3 shot template or full page single shot right from the interactive touch screen. From the large touchscreen guests can choose to text, email and/or print thier photos.
